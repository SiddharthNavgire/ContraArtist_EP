About ContraArtist

Presenting Siddharth RN's inaugural EP, a showcase of his boundless talent and captivating creativity. Prepare for an unparalleled journey through an array of emotions, as Siddharth RN delivers an immersive experience across a diverse range of tracks.

Welcome to "ContraArtist", a collection of four distinct compositions brimming with unique melodies and evocative lyrics, each crafted to evoke a myriad of feelings. Get ready to embark on a musical odyssey like no other, where every note is a testament to Siddharth RN's unparalleled artistry.

The EP is Releasing on this 4th May 2024 !!!

-- Track List --

Khayalo Mein

Introducing "Khayalo Mein," the opening anthem of this EP, radiating joyous love and boundless excitement. Delve into the euphoria of love at first sight, as the song captures the exhilarating rush of emotions experienced when hearts collide for the very first time.

No One Like You

Presenting "No One Like You," the poignant second installment of this EP, echoing the somber depths of heartbreak in love's tumultuous journey. Prepare to be moved by the raw emotion as the rap segment intensifies, striking a chord with those who relate to its visceral portrayal of emotional turmoil.

Sab Sahi Sajna

Introducing "Sab Sahi Sajna," a distinctive offering from Siddharth RN that promises to redefine your musical experience. Prepare to be enchanted by its unconventional charm, featuring a captivating hook and laid-back rap verses. Brace yourself for an emotional journey as "Sajna" comes to terms with the inevitable end.

Samandar

Presenting "Samandar," a soul-stirring ode from Siddharth RN to the divine, expressing gratitude for life's trials and triumphs. Through heartfelt lyrics, Siddharth RN offers himself to God, pledging to confront life's challenges with unwavering hope and unwavering honesty.
